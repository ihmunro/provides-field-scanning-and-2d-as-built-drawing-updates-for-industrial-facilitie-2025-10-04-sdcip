About AsBuilt

Founded in 2018, AsBuilt emerged from a vision to revolutionize industrial facility documentation. Our founders, with over 30 years of combined experience in industrial engineering and 3D scanning technology, recognized the critical need for more efficient and accurate as-built documentation services. What began as a local operation has grown into a trusted partner for major industrial facilities across North America.

Our mission is to deliver unparalleled accuracy and efficiency in facility documentation while minimizing operational disruption. We're driven by our core values of precision, innovation, and client success. Through continuous investment in cutting-edge technology and expert team development, we're committed to setting new industry standards for as-built documentation services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical turnaround time for deliverables?

Most projects are delivered within 5-7 business days after scanning, with rush options available for time-sensitive needs. Large facility scans may require additional processing time.

How do you ensure accuracy in your measurements?

We employ multi-phase verification using state-of-the-art scanning equipment and software, with redundant quality control checks. Our process guarantees accuracy to within 1mm for most applications.

Can you work around active operations?

Yes, our scanning process is designed to minimize interference with ongoing operations. We can schedule work during off-peak hours and coordinate with facility management to ensure minimal disruption.

What file formats do you provide?

We deliver in all major CAD formats including AutoCAD (.dwg), Revit (.rvt), and other industry-standard formats. Custom file formats are available upon request.

Do you offer ongoing support after delivery?

Yes, we provide 30 days of complimentary support after delivery, including revisions and technical assistance. Extended support packages are available for long-term projects.
